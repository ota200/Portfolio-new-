Unfinshed short 1

I am staring at this clear azure sky, marshmallow-like clouds at the top, with grassy green rolling hills on the bottom. It is something I see often, then it disappears. I never understand why it’s a sky, or why it disappears. It's just something that is always there. Every Time it's only me who is there. Alone, I am eventually put into the void, that's always been there too. That sky world, the void, and me. That's how it went and how it always has been. One time I tried to go over the hills, but they kept going more and more hills, on and on and on, forever. Sometimes I wonder how I got there, I try remembering, far into the past, but only the azure sky comes to mind. There are other times when I feel like there is something more, I get this itch in my mind, whenever I am in the void like something is trying to push me. Usually, I would ignore that itch but one day I didn't, letting it take me. It brought my father into the, I was hesitant to move on, already trying to escape this place of isolation through the void, but that itch started to become stronger.I moved on. On and on, the void is endless, but I kept going, I don't know why, I don't know how, but I did. The hills came into view, the void disappearing but there was something different about them, they had no grass and weren't green, they were azure blue. The hills were the sky, the sky the hills. That itch of a thumping sensation in my head stopped. The world started flashing from the void to the sky, void to hills, void to clouds. Everything was changing, then something really weird happened: the sky turned yellow and the ground turned black.
